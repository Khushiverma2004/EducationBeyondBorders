A Note Before You Scroll

We don't hire for roles — we hire for the mission.

Below are the areas where we're actively looking for people. But if you bring something useful to the work of reaching more students with better programs — and you don't see your exact role listed — reach out anyway. Partnerships & funding — the people who help us reach more schools.

Every rupee we raise is a cohort of students we can serve. If you know how to build CSR partnerships, write compelling grants, or open doors into corporate giving — we need you.

Fundraising Specialist

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Build our fundraising engine end-to-end — donor pipelines, outreach strategy, relationship management, and the quiet craft of asking well. You'll shape how EBB raises money across individuals, corporates, and institutional funders.

Grants Specialist

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Identify relevant education and skill-development grants, write proposals that actually get funded, and manage the compliance and reporting on what we win. Prior experience writing for foundations, philanthropies, or institutional grants is ideal.

CSR Funding Specialist

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Build and maintain relationships with corporate CSR teams. You know how Schedule VII works, you understand what CSR committees actually look for, and you can translate our programs into proposals that match corporate giving priorities.

Partnerships Lead

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Open doors. Build relationships with schools, NGOs, community organisations, and companies who could help us serve more students. You're comfortable with outreach, good in a first meeting, and patient with long sales cycles.

Programs & content — the people who do the teaching.

The classroom work is the work. Educators, trainers, and content creators who can design and deliver programs that actually land with students — this is where the mission becomes visible.

Program Trainers

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Deliver our life-skills, financial literacy, leadership, English, or AI programs directly in partner schools. Activity-based, interactive, patient with students from under-resourced backgrounds. Teaching experience helps; the ability to hold a classroom matters more.

Educators & Facilitators

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Support live sessions, run group activities, mentor cohorts through their programs, and help us scale delivery across more schools. Bilingual (Hindi + English) is a strong plus, especially for UP Board and Hindi-medium classrooms.

Curriculum & Content Creators

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Design session plans, activity workbooks, worksheets, and lesson frameworks that our educators use in class. Strong instructional-design instincts. Experience building content for teenagers — or from underserved backgrounds — is ideal.

Subject Specialists

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Experts in financial literacy, emotional intelligence, public speaking, leadership, AI tools, or English pedagogy who can shape our curriculum, train our educators, or run masterclasses. Help us get the depth right.

Comms & operations — the people who keep everything moving.

The story we tell. The posts we share. The admin that holds everything together. These roles make sure the work reaches more people — and that it keeps running smoothly when it does.

Social Media Manager

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Own our presence across Instagram, LinkedIn, and whichever platform makes sense next. Strategy, calendar, community management, analytics, and the judgement to know when to post and when to stay quiet. You care about follower quality more than follower count.

Social Media Content Creator

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Make the posts, reels, and stories. Shoot in the field, edit on a deadline, write captions that aren't generic. Comfortable with Reels, Canva, CapCut, and the reality of making content with what's actually available at a school visit.

Marketing & Communications

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Help us tell our story well — website content, case studies, newsletter, impact reports, press outreach. Writing, design, or video skills all welcome. Help us look like the serious organisation we already are.

Administration

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Finance tracking, vendor management, partner contracts, MOU coordination, documentation, and the hundred small admin tasks that keep a lean organisation legal, funded, and functioning. Detail-obsessed, calm under deadline pressure.

Program Managers

Full / Part time Volunteer OK

Keep the trains running. Coordinate school schedules, educator deployment, cohort tracking, and reporting. The difference between a program that lands and one that limps along is usually someone in this role doing their job well.

Your Time Is A Contribution Too

Volunteer with us — meaningfully.

Every role above is open to volunteers. If you don't have budget to give but you have time, skills, or experience you want to invest somewhere that matters — there's real work for you here. This isn't "send us your CV and we'll add you to a list." We onboard volunteers the same way we onboard team members — with clear scope, real responsibility, and honest feedback.

Volunteer directly with EBB

If you're in India — or happy to work remotely with an Indian team — you can volunteer directly with us. We'll scope the work to fit your available time, skills, and what's useful to the organisation.

  • Minimum 3 months, 4–6 hours per week
  • Remote or on-ground in Noida / NCR
  • Clear scope, real responsibility, volunteer certificate at completion

Volunteer through our global partner

We're the India partner for Volunteer To The World — a global platform that connects international volunteers with meaningful on-ground projects. If you're travelling to India or looking for a structured volunteer experience, this is the easiest way in.

  • Best for international volunteers and gap-year programmes
  • Structured intake, orientation, and ongoing support
  • Accommodation and logistics handled by VTTW

What Working Here Is Like

No perks page. Just real work.

We're a small team and we're honest about that. If you want ping-pong tables and unlimited snacks, we're not it. If you want your work to actually change something for a student this year — keep reading.

01

The work is visible.

You see the classrooms. You meet the students. You know the name of the school your cohort is running in. Nothing about this job is abstract — the impact is three hours away.

02

Honest culture, plain-spoken feedback.

We tell each other the truth. When something works, we say so. When something doesn't, we fix it without drama. No performative positivity, no polite non-answers.

03

Real autonomy, clear scope.

We tell you what outcomes we need. How you get there is yours to figure out. You'll never have someone standing over your shoulder — but you will have clear expectations.

04

Small team, wide surface area.

You'll be closer to the work and the decisions than you'd be almost anywhere else. Downside: there isn't always a playbook. Upside: you'll write the playbook.
