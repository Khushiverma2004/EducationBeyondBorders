ABOUT US

We align school realities with structured support.

Not goodwill. Not one-off drives. EBB builds partnerships that are practical, measurable, and designed to outlast the moment they were created.

OUR STORY

Born from a gap that everyone could see and no one had closed.

Resources exist in the corporate world. Needs are acute in schools. The bridge connecting them has always been fragile — built on goodwill, not systems.

EBB was built to change that. We don't facilitate donations. We architect partnerships — matching CSR mandates to structural school needs so programs run properly, deliver real outcomes, and keep running after the launch event is over.

Our Mission

To democratize quality education by creating scalable, accountable partnerships between schools and the organizations that want to meaningfully support them.

Our Vision

A world where a student's access to opportunity is not an accident of geography or funding.

IMPLEMENTATION

The 5-Step Approach

Good intentions without process produce nothing. Every EBB partnership follows a rigorous, repeatable methodology — from first assessment to final report.

01

Needs Assessment

Structured evaluation of school infrastructure, curriculum gaps, student demographics, and institutional capacity. We start with evidence, not assumptions.

02

Partner Alignment

We align partners whose CSR objectives, timelines, and capabilities fit the school's real needs — not just whoever responds first.

03

Program Design

Co-created with school leadership and delivery partners. Clear goals, defined timelines, assigned responsibilities, and measurable outcomes from day one.

04

Delivery & Monitoring

On-ground execution with live tracking of participation, delivery quality, and continuity. Problems get flagged and fixed — not buried in the next report.

05

Impact Reporting

Transparent, data-backed outcomes for every stakeholder. Schools see what changed. Partners see what their investment built. Students get proof of progress.
