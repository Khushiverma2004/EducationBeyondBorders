Our Story

Education that actually reaches the students who need it most.

EBB exists to close the gap between schools that need support and the people, organisations, and communities ready to provide it with programs built to last, not just look good on paper.

Our Story

We started because too many good intentions were going to waste.

In classrooms across India, teachers were stretched thin and students were often left to figure out life's biggest questions on their own. How to speak up, manage money, handle stress, lead a team, and imagine a better future.

At the same time, corporates, NGOs, and communities wanted to help, but the programs they funded often disappeared after the launch photo. The bridge between intent and actual implementation was always the problem.

EBB was built to become that bridge. Not a broker, but a hands-on partner. We design the programs, train the educators, track the outcomes, and stay long enough to see what actually changes for students.

Our Mission

To give every student access to the skills school was never designed to teach.

Confidence, communication, financial sense, emotional resilience, and the ability to imagine a different future. We build programs that reach students who would otherwise miss out entirely.

Our Vision

A generation where your starting point doesn't set your ceiling.

Where the school you attend, the income your family earns, or the city you were born in no longer decides the trajectory of your life. Opportunity should be built in, not something students have to hope for.

How We Work

Five steps, every single time.

Good intentions are not enough. Every program we run follows the same structured path so the work is practical, measurable, and repeatable across schools and partnerships.

01

We listen first.

Before anything else, we sit with school leadership, teachers, and students to understand the real gaps, not the ones that simply sound impressive in a proposal.

02

We align the right partner.

We connect schools with CSR partners, NGOs, or communities whose goals, resources, and timelines genuinely fit the school's needs. A weak match helps no one.

03

We design the programs.

Our curriculum is shaped with educators, psychologists, and subject experts. Every module is activity-based, grounded in what students actually respond to, and built around outcomes we can track.

04

We show up in the classroom.

Our trained educators deliver sessions consistently, week after week. We monitor attendance, engagement, and learning progress as the program runs, not after it ends.

05

We report honestly.

Schools see what changed for students. Partners see exactly where their support went. We share both wins and gaps because real impact cannot be told selectively.

What Guides Us

The values we actually use.

Not slogans on a wall. These are the standards we use when we decide what to build, who to partner with, and where our work should go next.

Transparency

Every session, every decision, and every outcome should be visible to the people trusting us with their time, money, and students.

Inclusivity

We build for the students schools usually struggle to reach, not the ones already thriving. If a program only works for the top of the class, it is not enough.

Innovation

Traditional systems were not built for these life-shaping skills, so we keep evolving how we teach, deliver, and measure what matters.

Empathy

Every student walks into the room with circumstances we may never fully see. Our responsibility is to meet them where they are, not where a system assumes they should be.
