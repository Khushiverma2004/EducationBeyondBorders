How We Work With Partners

What we always do and what we will not.

A partnership only works when both sides know the rules of engagement. Here are ours, upfront.

We will
  • Say no if we cannot deliver. If a timeline or budget will not produce real outcomes, we will tell you before we sign.
  • Report what went wrong. Quarterly reports include the things that did not work, not just the highlights.
  • Share raw data on request. Attendance logs, session notes, and assessment results are available to partners.
  • Credit partners honestly. Your contribution is visible in classrooms, certificates, and reports.
  • Stay accountable to students first. If a partner request conflicts with what is right for students, students win.
We will not
  • Run one-day drives or branding events. If the program is not structured, measurable, and sustained, we will not take the money.
  • Inflate numbers for optics. Our reports use actual reach, not vague beneficiary counts.
  • Use students as props. No posed photo opportunities, scripted testimonials, or classroom visits timed for PR.
  • Accept scope that does not fit the school. If the ask does not match what the school needs, the answer is no.
  • Overcommit to grow faster. We would rather serve 8 schools exceptionally well than 80 poorly.
